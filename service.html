Our Services
Coffee Farm Startup & Coffee Cuttings Supply
We hope establish coffee farms with the primary goal of producing and supplying high-quality coffee cuttings to farmers and agribusinesses. Our mission is to support coffee growers by providing disease- resistant, high-yield varieties that enhance productivity and sustainability.
Coffee Agro-Inputs Shop & Advisory Services
We intend to set up a Coffee Agro-Inputs Shop in Kayunga and Mukono to provide our smallholder farmers with high-quality agricultural products and expert advisory services at subsidized prices in order to enhance coffee productivity and profitability. Our goal is to support coffee growers with the right inputs and knowledge to achieve sustainable and high-yield farming.

Coffee aggregation and trading
We are dedicated to providing reliable coffee trading and aggregation services, connecting coffee farmers with local and international markets. Our goal is to ensure fair prices, improve market access, and enhance the value of Ugandan coffee through high-quality sourcing and efficient supply chain management.
Coffee Processing & Value Addition Services
We are passionate about coffee processing and value addition services that enhance quality, increase market value, and create premium coffee products. Our services include coffee drying, hulling and roasting to ensure high standards for local and international markets. Partner with us to transform raw coffee into high-quality, market-ready products! Include Link

Empowering the next Generation
We intend to launch a Coffee Skills Training School, dedicated to equipping young people with the knowledge and hands-on expertise needed to thrive in the coffee industry. Our goal is to create employment opportunities and build a skilled workforce for Uganda’s growing coffee sector. These trainings will include:
  • ● Barista Training
  • ● Coffee Roasting & Processing
  • ● Value Addition
  • ● Quality Assurance & Grading
  • ● Business & Entrepreneurship in Coffee

Impact Investment in Coffee – Grow Profits, Empower Communities
We offer a unique impact investment opportunity in the coffee sector, allowing our investors to earn profitable returns while making a meaningful difference in local communities. By investing in our coffee business units ranging from farming, processing, trading, and training, you contribute to a sustainable model where a percentage of profits is reinvested into farmer empowerment, youth training, and community development projects.
